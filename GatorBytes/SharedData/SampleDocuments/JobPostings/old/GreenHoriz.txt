**Position: Middle School Teacher (Grades 6-8)**
**Location:** Green Horizons Charter School, St. Louis, MO
**Schedule:** Hybrid (Alternating Weeks In-Person & Online)
**Start Date:** August 2024
**Application Deadline:** July 10, 2024
---
**About Us:**
Green Horizons Charter School is a vibrant, growing educational community entering our fourth year of service. Our charter school is committed to innovative, student-centered education, focusing on project-based learning and strong community values. We pride ourselves on fostering creativity, resilience, and respect across our diverse student body.
---
**Job Description:**
Green Horizons Charter School is seeking a passionate and flexible *Middle School Teacher* for the 2024-2025 academic year. The successful candidate will teach core middle school subjects (language arts, math, science, social studies) for grades 6-8. This is a unique, hybrid position: every other week, instruction and engagement take place remotely using our online platforms; in alternate weeks, teaching resumes in person at our state-of-the-art campus.
**Key Responsibilities:**
- Design and deliver engaging lessons in a hybrid (alternating in-person/online) environment.
- Assess, track, and report student progress in all core academic areas.
- Adapt curriculum and instruction to meet the academic, social, and emotional needs of a diverse student population.
- Maintain regular communication with families regarding student progress and classroom happenings.
- Utilize technology and online learning platforms to enhance instruction and promote student engagement during remote weeks.
- Create a positive, inclusive classroom culture centered on our school values.
- Participate in regular professional development and school-wide events.
- Collaborate actively with fellow teachers, administrators, and instructional coaches.
---
**Qualifications:**
- Bachelor’s degree (required); certification in Middle School or appropriate subject area (preferred, but not required for exceptional candidates).
- 2+ years teaching experience (middle school or upper elementary preferred).
- Experience with blended or online/hybrid learning strongly preferred.
- Comfort with learning management systems (Google Classroom, Zoom, or similar).
- Demonstrated ability to build positive relationships with students and families from diverse backgrounds.
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and organizational skills.
- Commitment to continuous improvement and professional growth.
---
**Compensation & Benefits:**
- Salary range: $46,000–$57,000 (commensurate with experience)
- Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, vision, and retirement
- Professional development opportunities
- Small class sizes (max 20 students)
- Supportive and innovative school culture
