About the Role

Apexion Global Technologies is seeking an experienced Lead C# Developer to take ownership of a mission critical, enterprise-grade platform that is currently behind schedule. You will lead the technical turnaround of this system, stabilize the codebase, and drive it to a production-ready state used by thousands of internal and external users worldwide.

This role is ideal for a senior engineer who is comfortable inheriting complex, partially delivered systems, diagnosing issues quickly, and putting structure, quality, and predictability back into the delivery process.

Key Responsibilities

  • Take end-to-end technical ownership of a delayed, mission critical C#/.NET application.
  • Analyze the current architecture, codebase, and data model; identify stability, scalability, and performance issues.
  • Define and execute a clear remediation and delivery plan to bring the project back on schedule.
  • Work hands-on with C#, Entity Framework, and SQL Server to refactor, optimize, and complete core features.
  • Collaborate with product managers, architects, QA, and infrastructure teams to align technical decisions with business priorities.
  • Introduce or strengthen engineering best practices: code reviews, branching strategy, CI/CD, automated testing, and release management.
  • Mentor and guide a small team of developers; provide technical direction and support day-to-day implementation decisions.
  • Improve observability (logging, metrics, tracing) to proactively detect and address production issues.
  • Contribute to and validate technical documentation, runbooks, and support guides.
  • Communicate status, risks, and trade-offs clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
