Front-End Developer (Angular)

HorizonLex Solutions · Mid-size B2B Software Company · Hybrid – Chicago, IL
Angular Front End Product Team Mid-level · Full-time
About the role

HorizonLex Solutions is a mid-size corporate technology company providing workflow and analytics tools to enterprise clients. We’re looking for a strong Front-End Developer to join our product engineering team and help evolve our flagship web platform.

In this role, you will work closely with other engineers, designers, and product managers to:

  • Enhance and maintain existing Angular applications used by our customers every day
  • Contribute to new product features and greenfield initiatives
  • Introduce and champion front-end best practices, patterns, and tooling
You’ll have significant impact on how we grow and modernize our front-end codebase, while keeping mission‑critical features stable and reliable.

What you’ll do

  • Build, refactor, and optimize user-facing features in our Angular applications.
  • Collaborate with UX/UI designers to translate wireframes and visual designs into clean, reusable components.
  • Expand on our existing design system and component library to support new product capabilities.
  • Work on both existing projects and new initiatives, balancing incremental improvements with new feature delivery.
  • Introduce and enforce best practices in front-end architecture, state management, testing, and performance.
  • Write well-structured, testable, and maintainable TypeScript and HTML/CSS.
  • Participate in code reviews, pair programming, and knowledge-sharing sessions with the team.
  • Collaborate with back-end teams to define and integrate RESTful API contracts.
  • Debug and resolve issues across supported browsers and devices, improving reliability and user experience.
  • Contribute to build and deployment pipelines (CI/CD) related to the front-end codebase.

Our tech stack

Core front-end technologies you’ll work with:

Angular (v12+) TypeScript RxJS NgRx or similar state management HTML5 CSS3 / SCSS RESTful APIs Jasmine / Karma / Jest Git CI/CD (Azure DevOps / GitHub Actions)

Experience with adjacent tools (Webpack, ESLint, Prettier, Storybook, etc.) is helpful but not required from day one.

What we’re looking for

  • 3+ years of professional experience as a Front-End or Web Developer, with at least 2 years using Angular in production.
  • Strong proficiency in TypeScript, modern JavaScript (ES6+), and core web technologies (HTML, CSS).
  • Solid understanding of Angular concepts: components, modules, services, dependency injection, routing, and forms.
  • Experience with reactive programming and RxJS (observables, operators, subjects).
  • Comfortable working in and improving a large existing codebase, not just greenfield projects.
  • Familiarity with front-end testing frameworks and practices (unit tests, basic integration tests).
  • Understanding of web performance, accessibility (a11y), and responsive design principles.
  • Ability to collaborate effectively in a cross-functional team (engineering, product, design, QA).
  • Clear, respectful communication skills—written and verbal.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field, or equivalent practical experience.

Nice to have

  • Experience with NgRx or another state management solution for Angular.
  • Exposure to design systems, component libraries, or Storybook.
  • Background in enterprise or B2B SaaS environments.
  • Experience working with RESTful back-end APIs built with .NET, Java, or Node.js.
  • Familiarity with Docker, Kubernetes, or cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, or GCP).
  • Experience introducing or formalizing best practices (linting, code style guides, testing strategies).

How we work

  • Hybrid schedule: 2–3 days per week in our downtown Chicago office, with flexibility.
  • Agile-inspired process with two-week sprints, daily stand-ups, and regular demos.
  • Emphasis on collaboration, mentorship, and continuous improvement.
  • Opportunity for growth into senior engineering or technical lead roles as the team expands.

Compensation & benefits

  • Competitive salary range: $105,000 – $130,000 USD, depending on experience and location.
  • Annual performance bonus eligibility.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage.
  • 401(k) with company match.
  • Generous PTO, sick leave, and paid holidays.
  • Professional development budget for courses, conferences, and certifications.
  • Modern equipment and software tools of your choice (within standard options).
