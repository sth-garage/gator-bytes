Orion Global Solutions is a Fortune 500 technology and services company powering mission-critical operations for clients in finance, healthcare, and logistics across 40+ countries. We are seeking an experienced Front-End Team Lead to own and evolve the UI for our flagship enterprise product suite.

In this role, you will lead a small but growing front-end team, modernize a large-scale React application, address legacy AngularJS modules, and champion front-end best practices across the organization.

You will be the senior-most front-end engineer on the product, with direct influence over architecture, standards, and the technical direction of the UI platform.

What you will do

  • Lead a team of 4–6 front-end engineers delivering features for a high-traffic enterprise web application.
  • Own and evolve our React-based front-end architecture, establishing patterns, standards, and guidelines.
  • Define and roll out front-end best practices: code quality, testing strategies, performance, accessibility, and CI/CD.
  • Plan and oversee the gradual migration of key legacy AngularJS modules to modern React/TypeScript.
  • Collaborate with product, UX, and back-end teams to plan and deliver complex, multi-quarter initiatives.
  • Conduct design and code reviews, provide ongoing technical coaching, and grow the capabilities of the team.
  • Partner with Architecture and Platform Engineering to improve our design system, component library, and tooling.
  • Monitor and improve application performance, bundle size, and perceived responsiveness.
  • Drive quality through automated testing (unit, integration, and end-to-end) and robust release practices.
  • Represent front-end engineering in cross-functional planning and architecture forums.

What we are looking for

Must-have qualifications

  • 10+ years of professional experience in front-end or full-stack web development.
  • 3+ years in a formal leadership capacity (team lead, tech lead, or engineering manager).
  • Deep expertise in React, modern JavaScript (ES6+), and component-based architectures.
  • Practical experience working with or migrating from AngularJS (1.x).
  • Strong proficiency with HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design for complex web applications.
  • Hands-on experience with TypeScript in production systems.
  • Proven track record of introducing and enforcing engineering best practices in a large codebase.
  • Experience working in a large corporate or enterprise environment with multiple stakeholders.
  • Ability to design scalable front-end architectures and API integration patterns.
  • Excellent communication skills and the ability to influence and mentor engineers at different levels.

Nice-to-have experience

  • Background with state management libraries (Redux, Zustand, or similar) and query libraries (React Query, SWR).
  • Experience with design systems and component libraries (Storybook, internal UI kits, Material UI, Chakra, etc.).
  • Familiarity with Node.js build tooling (Webpack, Vite, Babel, ESLint, Prettier) and CI/CD pipelines.
  • Exposure to micro-frontend architectures or modular monolith front-ends.
  • Solid understanding of accessibility standards (WCAG) and building inclusive user interfaces.
  • Experience in regulated industries (finance, healthcare, insurance, or government) is a plus.

How you work

  • You are comfortable operating in and improving a large existing codebase rather than only greenfield work.
  • You think in systems and standards, not one-off solutions, and advocate for long-term maintainability.
  • You value code review, documentation, and knowledge sharing as part of your daily practice.
  • You can break down complex initiatives into clear, prioritized milestones for your team.
  • You balance technical excellence with business impact and can articulate trade-offs to stakeholders.
