Junior C# Developer

Northbridge Solutions · Mid-size Product Company
Hybrid · Austin, TX (2–3 days on-site) Full-time New Product Development Team Mentored / Early Career

About Northbridge Solutions

Northbridge Solutions is a mid-size software company (≈180 employees) focused on building modern B2B platforms for operations and analytics. We are kicking off a greenfield product in 2025 and are expanding our engineering team with a motivated Junior C# Developer who wants to learn, grow, and ship real features.

You will join a cross-functional team of senior engineers, a product manager, and a UX designer working collaboratively to build our next-generation SaaS application from the ground up.

The Role

As a Junior C# Developer, you will work closely with experienced developers who will provide mentorship and code reviews while you:

  • Implement new backend features in C# as part of a modern web application.
  • Write and maintain SQL queries, stored procedures, and data access code.
  • Use an ORM such as Entity Framework or Dapper to work with relational data.
  • Contribute to RESTful APIs consumed by our web front-end.
  • Participate in code reviews, backlog grooming, and sprint planning.
  • Write basic unit/integration tests and help debug production issues with guidance.

This role is ideal for someone with foundational C# knowledge (academic, bootcamp, or 0–2 years industry experience) who wants to learn best practices and work on a new, evolving product.
